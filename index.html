Seagull

The larger species — in particular — are resourceful, inquisitive and intelligent birds, many gull colonies display mobbing behaviour, attacking and harassing would-be predators and other intruders.

  • Class — Avus
  • Family — Laridae
  • Size — up to 66 cm long
  • Wingspan — 1,2 – 1,6 m
  • Weight — 1 – 1,5 kg

The Seagull (Laridae) is a large seabird — one of the best known of all gulls along the shores of Europe and the Baltic states. The Laridae a varied diet, including fish, crustaceans and dead animals as well as some plants.
